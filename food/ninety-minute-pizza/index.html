Ingredients

  • 3.5 cups bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 10 grams instant yeast

Ninety Minute Pizza

Instructions

  1. Make Dough

    Warm beer in saucepot over low heat. Combine the bread flour, sugar, yeast and kosher salt in a bowl and combine. Add the beer and 2 tablespoons of the oil and mix until the dough forms into a ball. If the dough is sticky, add additional flour, 1 tablespoon at a time, until the dough comes together in a solid ball. If the dough is too dry, add additional water, 1 tablespoon at a time. Scrape the dough onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead into a smooth, firm ball.

  2. Make Sauce

    Combine all ingredients and pulse with an immersion blender until smooth, about 30 sec.

  3. Move & Prove

    Transfer dough into a well oiled 12‚Äù cast iron. With oiled fingers, press dough out to fill the shape of the pan. Cover and allow to prove for 10 minutes.

  4. Sauce

    Spread 4-5 generous spoonfuls of sauce over the dough all the way to the edges.

  5. Cheese

    Sprinkle Montery Jack around the edges. The outside edge will puff up while baking and become part of the top crust, and become engulged by the Montery Jack. Fill in the middle with mozzarella

  6. Top & Bake

    Top with whatever toppings you'd like - I like onions and Mark likes pepperoni so that's what we go with, but you do you.

  7. Slice

    Slice starting from the middle, then move towards the outside edge.
