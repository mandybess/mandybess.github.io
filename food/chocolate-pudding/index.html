Ingredients

  • ¼ cup packed (1¾ ounces) light brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons Dutch-processed cocoa powder
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 ¾ cups whole milk
  • ¼ cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup (6 ounces) milk chocolate chips
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Chocolate Pudding 🍫

Instructions

  1. Combine the dry (brown sugar, cocoa, cornstarch, and salt) and wet (milk and cream) ingredients into a saucepan and whisk until smooth.
  2. Add chocolate chips and bring to simmer, whisking occasionally, over medium heat.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low and cook, whisking constantly, until thickened and large bubbles appear at surface, 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Off heat, stir in vanilla.
  5. Transfer pudding to large bowl and place plastic wrap directly on surface of pudding.
  6. Refrigerate until completely cool, at least 4 hours.

  1. 🥶 If you want to eat this sooner, you can speed up chilling by placing the pudding in a low baking dish, and freezing for one hour.
